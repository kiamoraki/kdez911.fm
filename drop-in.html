KDEZ is small but mighty. And growing fast.

If you want to come see us, you'll need to make your way to Bombay Beach, a strange town on the North Shore of the Salton Sea.

Until then, join our mailing list. We'll tell you about new shows as they hit the airwaves, free workshops on how to make the radio, and a Radio Rodeo or two.

We'll send you an email right away with ways to tune in and get involved with a link to join our Discord and get on air.
