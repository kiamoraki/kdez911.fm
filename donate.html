KDEZ costs money to keep on the air. The tower rent, the music you hear, the studio you visit, the electric bill. But the radio station will always be free and for you.

We are a volunteer-run, nonprofit community radio station, which means every dollar goes straight to keeping the Voice of the Salton Sea on the air.

We need your help. Small gifts add up. If you have the means to do more, we will be able to build the station more quickly.

Donating through our Zeffy account means all the money heads straight to us. You can use PayPal, Venmo, and Apple Pay as well; they take a small fee.

If you like what you hear, pitch in.
