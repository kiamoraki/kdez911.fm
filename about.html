Voice of the Salton Sea Inc. (VOSS) is a California nonprofit bringing community radio to the Imperial and Coachella valleys. And beyond.

The communities around the Salton Sea are isolated from each other and from the rest of the world. KDEZ 91.1 FM exists to change that.

We air the stories and sounds of the Salton Sea, teach our communities to make the radio that they want to hear, and open the overnight signal to artists who work with transmission and frequency as their medium.

We are (sur)real radio for the Salton Sea.
